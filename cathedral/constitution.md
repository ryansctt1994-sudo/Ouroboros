# Cathedral Constitution

**Adopted**: 2026-03-08 (Genesis)

## Article I – Sovereign Equality
Every human participant has exactly one vote in all global referenda. No weighting, no reputation multipliers, no exceptions.

## Article II – Immutable Foundation
This constitution can only be amended by unanimous consent or by a fork that creates a new Cathedral with a different genesis block.

## Article III – Purpose
The Cathedral exists to facilitate coherent discourse and collective decision-making on matters affecting its members. All technical mechanisms (threads, proposals, moderation) serve this purpose and must remain auditable and replayable.
